ng-backstretch

Based off Scroll Robins jquery-backstretch



Works on any div, including backgrounds: 


          <div style='position: fixed; top: 0; left: 0; width: 100%; height: 100%; z-index: -1' >
            <div backstretch fillheight='1200' fillsrc="'{{useBG}}'" fade='500'></div>
          </div>
